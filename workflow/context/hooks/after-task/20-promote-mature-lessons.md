# Promote Mature Lessons

`after-task`

## Rules

- Promote only stable, reusable lessons into `workflow/context/experience/` (stage/, tool/, risk/ 等子目录) or formal rules.
- 经验写入目标路径固定为 `workflow/context/experience/`，不写入 `workflow/state/experience/`（后者仅为加载规则索引）。
- After each stage, decide whether mature experience should now be fused into the workflow.
